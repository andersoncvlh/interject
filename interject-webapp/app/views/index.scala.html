This prototype demonstrates how a mixture of preservation actions can be smoothly integrated into the search infrastructure of the UK Web Archive, by acting as access helpers for end users.

By allowing our visitors to decide between the available options, the real needs of our designated communities can be expressed directly to us and so taken into account as part of the preservation planning process.

Developed at the UK Web Archive, as part of the SCAPE Project.
